NPM Millwork Inc. We are a Miami-based millwork company that specializes in the manufacture of high-quality cabinets. The company's shareholders have a long history of working with wood, having previously owned their own sawmill in Ukraine. A combination of European (Ukraine, German, Italian, and Spanish) and American furniture manufacturing experience gives to us a unique perspective on the furniture industry, and allows them to offer their customers a wide range of high-quality products. We are committed to ours reputation and goes above and beyond to ensure that every customer is satisfied. The company has a customer satisfaction rating of 98%.
For our wooden products we uses only the finest materials in the construction of its cabinets, including solid wood, veneers, and high-quality hardware. Our's cabinets are made to order, so customers can choose the exact style, finish, and features that they want.
NPM Millwork offers a wide range of cabinet styles, from traditional to contemporary. The company also has a team of experienced designers who can help customers create the perfect cabinetry for their home. In addition to cabinets, NPM Millwork also manufactures other millwork products, such as doors, wall cladding with decorative materials, saunas and pool lining, and molding, etc. The company can also provide custom millwork solutions for specific projects.
NPM Millwork is committed to providing its customers with the highest quality products and service. The company has a team of experienced craftsmen who are dedicated to creating beautiful and functional cabinetry. If you are looking for high-quality cabinets, NPM Millwork is the company to call. The company always provides excellent products and service to customers.
NPM MILLWORK INC owns its own workshop with state-of-the-art equipment and a well-organized production process. This guarantees that your furniture will be made to the highest standards and delivered on time, from the moment the order is placed to the installation of the furniture in your home.
